President and Chief Executive

Biography

Teresa Freeborn joined Xceed Financial Credit Union in September of 2006 as President and Chief Executive. Freeborn oversees all aspects of the Credit Union’s operations and represents Xceed globally. With thirteen Financial Centers in five states, Xceed currently serves 70,000 members with nearly $1 billion in assets under management.

Freeborn brings 40 years of credit union experience across the breadth of the credit union system. Before joining Xceed, Teresa was a member of Kinecta Federal Credit Union’s executive staff, serving as Senior Vice President of Marketing & Communications as well as President of Kinecta Financial and Insurance Services. Prior to moving to the United States, Freeborn was an executive with Central One Credit Union in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Freeborn is an active advocate in the credit union movement. She is a member of the Research Council for the Filene Research Institute, and immediate past chair of the California Credit Union League’s Board of Directors. She is a member of the Credit Union for Kids Advisory Board – Childrens Hospital Los Angeles, and participates on the annual Wine Auction Committee. A member of the Board of Directors for the Credit Union Executive Society (CUES), she was recently inducted into the 2014 CUES Hall of Fame for lifetime achievement and contributions to the credit union movement. She is also a member of the Western CUNA Management School Board of Trustees and the Board of Directors of the Richard Myles Johnson Foundation. A champion for World Council of Credit Unions (WOCCU), she was an inaugural member in WOCCU’s Global Women’s Leadership Network and is a member of WOCCU’s World Leadership Development Committee. Freeborn was named one of the industry’s “women to watch” by Credit Union Times in 2012, and was awarded the California Credit Union League’s Distinguished Service Award recognizing her current and ongoing commitment to the credit union community.

A native of Vancouver, British Columbia, Freeborn holds an MBA from Simon Fraser University and currently resides in Palos Verdes Estates, California.
