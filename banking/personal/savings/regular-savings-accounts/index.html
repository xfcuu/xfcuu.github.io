Regular Savings Accounts

Save for a brighter future.

Whatever you’re saving for—a special purchase, dream wedding, down payment, or something else—you’ll reach your goals faster with an Xceed savings account.

  • $5 minimum opening deposit
  • Dividends paid quarterly on balances of $100 or more
  • Use as overdraft protection for your Xceed Financial checking account
  • Make easy deposits through Payroll Deduction

Remember, it's easy to access your savings account anytime, anywhere with Xceed Online Banking and the Xceed Mobile App.

Stop by your local Xceed Financial Center, or call us at 951.413.9933 to open your savings account today!
